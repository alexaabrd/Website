What makes a GREAT BASEBALL PLAYER - the 3 A'S:
  • GREAT ATTITUDE - Love the game ; Want the ball
  • GREAT AGGRESSIVENESS - Lay Out ; Get Dirty; Take the Extra Base; Charge The Ball; Good pitch- Take A Rip
  • GREAT ANTICIPATION- Stay a Pitch/Play Ahead


3 L's
  • Listen to the Coach
  • Lay Out- Be Aggressive
  • Love the Game- Give It All You Got & It Will Give back To

HITTING - 4 R's
  • Rest
  • Ready
  • Rock

3 UNPARDONABLE SINS OF HITTING!!!!
  • Don't Move Your Head
  • Don't Be on Your Heels
  • Got to keep your hands back


5 T's of Hitting:
  • Tuck
  • Throw
  • Top Hand Drive
  • Turn the Hips
  • Twist the Feet

MOST IMPORTANT POINT IN HITTING!!!! Watch the Ball- Release, Rotation , Location

CHIN POSITION- Front Shoulder to Back Shoulder

MOST EXCITING HIT IS A TRIPLE- Hustle, Speed, Power

Finish the Swing- Stay on the Diving Board (Balance) & Pose for Sport's Illustrated

BUNTING
  • More desire than skill
  • Catch the ball with the barrel
  • Belly to the pitcher
  • Cushion the catch
  • Bat in the eyes
  • Don't move the bat ; bend the knees


5 T's of PITCHING
Take The Time To Tuck


THROWING
  • Grip the handles: the laces
  • Pressure point- fingertips
  • Extension out the front,/Extension out the back
  • Keep front side closed
  • Palms down
  • 3 P's - Push Pull Pop
  • You throw with your feet ; Good crowhop makes your arm stronger
  • Remember: Front side is your gunsite. Back side is your power


FIELDING STANCE:

Feet:
If playing up (corner infielders)- feet square
If back (middle inf.'s & OF's) - Play Deep and Creep
Hands:
Alligator mouth; glove open to hitter

3 Things in Glove
  1. Ball
  2. Throwing Hand
  3. Bill of cap

CATCHING:
Above waist- thumbs in
Below waist- thumbs out


Ground Balls
  • Look for candy -easiest way to catch a ground ball is in the air.
  • Charge it; 1st possible bounce.
  • Extend arms; catch ball out in front.
  • Be an alligator; eat the ball.
  • Cushion the catch; stab your throwing side.
  • As you make the catch, throwing foot is back; allows for crowhop step.


Fly Balls
  • Get your face behind the ball.
  • Be moving forward on the catch.
